Anagram Puzzle

Help! Parth had written a bunch of words for you that nicely spelled out the next clue but then Michael came and shuffled them all out of order. Can you put them together correctly?

Note: You could use Python to solve this, but you don't have to! Have fun with this! If you want to use Python, there's a list of all the words in the English language at our website, https://stanfordpython.com/res/misc/words.

Each line below is a word that's been shuffled out of order. Put the words back in order and then look at the first character of each word. Add .json to that and that'll be the location of the next puzzle.


                ntovesir
                legrovvni
                olifayilfc
                uuernrt
                ptmesreinirt
                nnersayivar
                onobkote

For example, if the words, unshuffled, were: 


                apple
                banana
                carrot
                donut
                egg
                fig
                grapefruit
                honey
then your next puzzle would be located at abcdefgh.json (well, actually https://stanfordpython.com/row-of-puzzles/abcdefgh.json, but you know what I mean).
